Odoo Tamara Payment Connect

Pay securely with full safety and convenience!!

Odoo Tamara Payment Connect enables your customers to make a safe and secure online payment on the Odoo website itself. They can just log in to their Tamara Payment Connect account and use the details saved into their accounts.

Detailed Features List

Enable Tamara Payment Connect payment gateway on your Odoo website

  • Facilitates single step integration of Tamara Payment Connect with Odoo website.
  • The customers can pay using Tamara Payment Connect for their Odoo website orders.
  • You can enable this reliable payment gateway on your Odoo website and provide ease to your customers.

Safe and reliable payment gateway

  • The 3D secure verification is provided while making payment online.
  • It is one of the safe and secure online payment gateways.
  • Provides smooth end to end payment flow.

Support different cards for payment from customers

  • Tamara Payment Connect payment gateway supports various debit/credit cards supported within KSA & UAE for payment from customers.
  • Tamara Payment Connect payment gateway provides better security management using card verification code.

Manage the Tamara Payment Connect payment method availability on Odoo website from the backend

  • From the back panel, you can easily enable/disable Tamara Payment Connect payment method.
  • You can easily add or remove this payment gateway from your Odoo website as per your wish.

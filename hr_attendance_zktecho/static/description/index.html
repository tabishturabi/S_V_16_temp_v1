User Guide

Open your employees menu, and navigate to the form view of one of your employees or create a new employee

Create a contract for your employee and make sure to assign the working schedule in the contract.

Go to Attendances -> Configuration -> Biometric Device menu to set up your first Device. Fill in your device information then press on "Test Connection".

Congratulations! You're in!

You must map the same ID # present on your Biometric Device onto the employee form view under the "Attendance ID" field.

Navigate to Attendances -> Attendances to see when your employees checked in and out, and even how many hours they have worked!

If some of your employees forgot to sign out or even to sign in you can generate those missing attendances through the "Generate Missing Attendances" menu.

Fetch your employees attendances and you can view them on the "Draft Attendances" menu, you can even create or edit attendances where appropriate. You can even filter, and group by attendances however you like.

Once you're done with the "Draft Attendances" you can move them to the Attendances tab through the "Move Attendance" menu. You can even move one employee at a time!
